# RAPTOR smoke benchmark (2026-07-12)

## Dataset

- Agency: Nagoya City Bus (`nagoya-cbus`)
- Stops: 3,886
- Trips: 30,417
- Feed version: `2026-05-18T01:57:40.761365`
- Snapshot: [`config/raptor-smoke-cases.json`](../../config/raptor-smoke-cases.json)

The cases were checked against the published GTFS and the Nagoya City Transportation
Bureau [bus timetable](https://www.kotsu.city.nagoya.jp/rp/bus/timetable.html). The
after-midnight case uses the published
[Kikan 2 Sakae to Shikenya route](https://www.kotsu.city.nagoya.jp/Yomiage/CityBusRoute/RosenMapKikanDetail?code=2&direction=%E5%BE%80&from=%E6%A0%84&name=%E5%9F%BA%E5%B9%B92&to=%E5%9B%9B%E8%BB%92%E5%AE%B6):
the 23:30 service reaches Kouribashi-higashi at 24:00 and Shikenya at 24:23.

## Results

Each measurement used two warmup runs followed by ten measured runs.

| Origin | Service date | Departure | Reachable stops | Representative arrivals | Median |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|---:|
| 栄 | 2026-07-07 | 08:00 | 3,884 | 名古屋駅 08:15, 金山 08:21 | 2.93 ms |
| 名古屋駅 | 2026-07-04 | 23:30 | 100 | 栄 unreachable, 金山 unreachable | 0.31 ms |
| 香流橋東 | 2026-07-08 | 00:00 | 3,884 | 四軒家 00:23, 栄 06:26 | 2.87 ms |

All cases passed the CLI sanity checks. The worst median was 2.93 ms, below the
200 ms target. Measurements used Node.js 26.5.0 on the local development machine;
CI remains responsible for correctness on the repository's supported Node.js 22.
